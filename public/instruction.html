Instructions

To explore and change the Digital Divide:

1. Hover over a country to view its name, National Technological Rank (in 2011) and IDI (in 2011).

2. Double click on the map to zoom in.

3. Double click again or press escape to zoom out.

4. Click a country to select it. Note: The country will not remain outlined if the mouse is no longer hovering over it, but the program has registered your selection.

5. Use the dropdown menus to specify the amount of each type of resource (computers, internet, mobile phones, broadband, and education) that you wish to “send” to the selected country.

6. Click the send button to “send” the resources.

7. Hover over the selected country again to view the updated IDI value and compare it to the country’s original IDI (in 2011).

8.If the IDI has increased enough, the color of the country will change to reflect where it now falls with regard to the Digital Divide.

9. Notice that the “Cost” has increased to reflect the total amount of money spent on sending resources.

10. To continue sending resources to the selected country, repeat steps 5-9.

11. To select a new country, simply click on the desired country and follow steps 4-9. Note: The “Cost” does not reset for each country but continues to increase until the entire map is reset.

12. To reset the map, return all IDI values to their original value and all countries to their original colors, click the reset button.
